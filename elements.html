Vaccine Reminder App

VNVC center is the largest provider of vaccination services in the Viet Nam. They need a phone application that helps remind vaccination schedules and manage their customer vaccination profiles.

Case study: UX research + UX design
App: Figma

Research

The problem

  • Customers forget their vaccination schedule and have no place to review their vaccination history
  • Waiting for check in is too long
  • The main customer is a child who cannot use the service by themselves.

The goal

  • Design an app to help remind customers of vaccination schedules, easily book vaccination schedules and manage vaccination history
  • Add the ability to manage multiple accounts

My role

UX research & UX design.

Responsibilities

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.
